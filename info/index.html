What is this website for?

This website was initially made for lurkers who like clicking on random links. Still, more specifically, it's targeted towards employers as it's essentially the portal to my CV/Resume and Portfolio and my socials. Even if you're not an employer and just a stranger on the internet, you are welcome to look at my hard work!

How did you make it?

I did not use any web designer. This website is purely made of HTML, CSS, and JS. I had planned my website a month before making it, and using web designer tools became impractical and more complicated for what I wanted to achieve. It would also be ironic for a programmer like myself to use no-code web designers. This website took around 30-ish total hours to complete over the course of a month. It was really fun to make, and I highly encourage others to make their own website!
